The United Kingdom & India all set to launch a Young Professionals Exchange Program in 2023

The UK will give 3000 bachelor's degree-holding students between the ages of 18 and 30 the chance to work there for two years. The Young Professional Exchange Programme is scheduled to debut in 2023. The bilateral lobby is scheduled to open in the first half of 2023. This was declared at the G20 Conference in Indonesia by the prime ministers of the UK, Rishi Sunak, and India, Narendra Modi.

For the first time, Indian and British nationals have reached an agreement that would improve the mobility rules for young professionals and allow them to live and work in both nations for up to two years.

The first "visa-national country" to benefit from a UK project of this kind is India. This underlines the robustness of the agreement on migration and mobility that the UK and India reached last year.

When can I apply for the UK Graduate Route visa?

After receiving confirmation from your university that you have successfully completed your studies, you can only apply for the UK Graduate Route visa. The UK government states that you can submit an application as soon as your final course results are handed to you; you are not need to wait until you have graduated.

It's crucial that you are in the UK when you submit your application because if you do so from abroad, your graduate visa will be rejected, and you risk losing your eligibility to apply for the route altogether. While you wait for the outcome of your application, be sure to remain in the UK because doing otherwise could damage your eligibility for the route and later re-entry into the nation.

You shouldn't be concerned about your current Tier 4 or student visa's expiration date. You will be able to remain in the UK until a decision is reached even if it expires after you've applied for the graduate route. Normally, you'll learn the outcome of your application eight weeks after you submit it.

How do I apply for the UK Graduate route visa?

There are two ways to apply for the UK Graduate route visa: online or in person.

The UK Graduate Route visa can be applied for in person or online. You must go to the GOV.UK website to complete the online application procedure. There, you can find instructions on how to register for a UK Visas and Immigration account and use a mobile app to prove your identification. You'll need your most recent visa authorization—which you would have gotten when you arrived in the UK to pursue your studies—on hand for this. Depending on your place of origin, you may receive an eVisa or a new biometric residency card if your application is approved.

Unlocking Adventures on a Budget: Student Travel Offers in the UK

Being a student is a wonderful stage of life that is full of learning, development, and adventure. The vibrant landscapes and towns of the United Kingdom are the perfect complement to your academic adventure. Fortunately, the UK provides a wide range of student-specific travel packages and discounts. We'll examine some of the amazing travel opportunities that await you as a student traveller in the UK in more detail in this blog.

The valued Rail-card is one of the most recognisable travelling companions for college students in the UK. The 16-25 Railcard gives full-time students between the ages of 16 and 25 and mature students between the ages of 26 and 35 a 1/3 discount on most rail fares nationwide. This card is your pass to touring the UK by train without breaking the bank, whether you're planning a weekend escape to a historic city or a picturesque excursion through the countryside.

The Oyster Card is a crucial tool for students living in London or those who want to experience the rich culture of the city. With a 30% discount on adult-rate transit cards and season tickets for the Bus & Tram Pass, the 18+ Student Oyster Photocard, your daily commute and weekend excursions through the city are now more affordable. The Oyster Card makes sure you're always prepared to go off on your next adventure, whether you're exploring the depths of the London Underground or boarding a double-decker bus to see the city's famous monuments.

Navigating the Tier 4 Visa: Your Gateway to Studying in the UK

International students who want to pursue undergraduate or graduate studies in the UK should apply for a Tier 4 Student Visa. This visa gives you the freedom to work part-time during your studies while allowing you to study full-time in the UK.

Eligibility:

You require an offer from an accredited educational institution in the UK in order to apply for a Tier 4 Visa.

Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS):

An exclusive reference number called a CAS will be given out by your university. To apply for the Tier 4 Visa, you must have this.

Financial Requirement:

You'll need to provide proof that you have enough money to pay your tuition and living costs in the UK.

Online Application:

Apply online for the Tier 4 Visa through the UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) website.

Pay Fees:

Pay the visa application fee and the Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) for access to the National Health Service (NHS).

Biometric Appointment:

Book an appointment to provide biometric information (fingerprints and photo) at a visa application center(VFS).

Documents Submission:

Submit required documents, including your CAS, financial proof, and other supporting materials.

Decision and Collection:

Wait for a decision on your application. Once approved, collect your biometric residence permit (BRP) after arriving in the UK.
